The Experience

Most Colosseum tours stay on the top two tiers. This one doesn't. With special evening access arranged through the Parco Archeologico, you'll descend into the hypogeum — the underground network where gladiators and animals waited before they were hoisted up through trapdoors. Then onto the reconstructed arena floor, standing where 50,000 Romans once looked down.
